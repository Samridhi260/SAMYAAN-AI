How It Works

1. Upload or Capture

Use your webcam or upload an image of a person you want to identify.

2. Face Detection

We use MTCNN for detecting faces, even with helmets or scarves.

3. Embedding + Prediction

FaceNet converts the face into an embedding, and a trained SVM identifies the person.

4. Result Displayed

You’ll immediately see the result below the image — with the identified name.

About SAMYAAN-AI

SAMYAAN-AI is a modern facial recognition system that combines AI accuracy with a user-friendly interface. Powered by MTCNN, FaceNet and SVM, it ensures secure and real-time identification.
